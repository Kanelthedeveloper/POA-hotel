About our Hotel

Our Hotel

We offer comfortable and versatile bedrooms as well as indoor leisure facilities. Our friendly customer focus is exceptional. And with our ‘can do’ attitude, we are happy to go the extra mile to make sure every guest has a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Prince of Anthony Hotel is built with style and comfort in mind, it’s got unique rooms each with its own individual and modern look, and we can proudly offer luxury accommodation which is stylish, sleek and original. We offer you the opportunity to indulge in a discounted accommodation with all the luxuries you will expect from a four star hotel. Come and enjoy our relaxing full-service hotel with everything you want at your beck and call.
